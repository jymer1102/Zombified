# Zombified
Zombie Shooter to Play at School
	•	Levels of difficulty
	⁃	1 through 5
	1.	Easy Zombie - Player gets knife
	2.	Knife Zombie - Player gets pistol
	3.	Knife Zombie and Bigger/Stronger Zombie - Player gets grenade
	4.	Knife Zombie and Bigger/Stronger Zombie - Player gets AR
	5.	Knife Regular Zombies and Biggest Strongest Boss Zombie

	•	Ammo Spread out across map
	•	Buildings to go through
	•	Ladders and stairs to go up
	•	Toggle "F" for headlamp
	•	Toggle "Q" for grenade
	•	"WASD" to move
	•	Mouse to look
	•	Left click - Shoot
	•	Right click - Aim
	•	Toggle "R" for knife
	•	Toggle "Z" to switch between pistol and AR
	•	Toggle "Esc" to pause - hold or press twice to exit fullscreen
	•	Toggle "Shift" to sprint
	•	Toggle "Ctrl" to crouch - hold to go prone
	•	Toggle "Ctrl" while sprinting to slide
	•	Kill 10, 15, 25, 30, 50 zombies progressively each level
	•	Singleplayer
	•	Zombies can't hear you come up behind them if you are crouching with headlamp turned off
	•	120° FOV for player and zombies
	•	First-person POV
	•	Carry 5 grenades max
